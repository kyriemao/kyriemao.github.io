About

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence, Renmin University of China. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Zhicheng Dou. Previously, I received M.Eng and B.Eng degrees in Computer Science from Tsinghua University in 2021 and from Nankai University in 2018, respectively.

I am working on generative information retrieval and LLMs.
