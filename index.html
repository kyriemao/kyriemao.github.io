About

I am a second-year PhD student at the Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence, Renmin University of China. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Zhicheng Dou. Previously, I recieved M.Eng and B.Eng degrees in Computer Science from Tsinghua University in 2021 and Nankai University in 2018, respectively.

My research interests are information retrieval, natural language processing, and recommender systems. I've been mainly working on the following research topics and projects:

  • Conversational Search: Developing methods for accurate question understanding and passage retrieval in the complex dialogue context. I worked on improving conversational dense retrieval models learn to denoise irrelevant turns of the context (SIGIR'22), alleviating the data scarcity problem to support the training of conversational dense retrieval models (EMNLP'22), developing an efficient and effective lexical-based conversational retriever, and developing conversational query rewriting models augmented with downstream retrieval information (Ongoing).
  • Recommender Systems: Developing simple yet effective and efficient recommendation models w.r.t collaborative filtering, CTR prediction, etc. I worked on developing a simple two-stream MLP-based model for CTR prediction (AAAI'23), an ultra simplification of GNN for efficient and effective collaborative filtering (CIKM'21), and proposing a simple unified collaborative filtering model with a new cosine contrastive loss (CIKM'21).

I also have broad interests in NLP/IR/Interdisciplinary ML: ML for network traffic classification (IWQoS'21) and ML for chemical retrosynthesis prediction (NeuroComputing'21), and textual item tagging with graph neural networks (SIGIR'20).

I’m looking for overseas (US/Japan/Singapore/Canada/EU) research (engineer) internship opportunities.
