About

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at the Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence, Renmin University of China. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Zhicheng Dou. Previously, I received M.Eng and B.Eng degrees in Computer Science from Tsinghua University in 2021 and from Nankai University in 2018, respectively.

I am dedicated to developing advanced information distribution systems. My research covers information retrieval, conversational search, RAG, large language models, recommender systems, etc.
