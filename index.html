Kelong Mao (毛科龙)

I am a first-year Joint Ph.D. student at Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence (GSAI) in Renmin University of China, and Department of Computer Science and Operations Research (DIRO) in Université de Montréal, supervised by Prof. Zhicheng Dou and Prof. Bang Liu, respectively.

Before that, I got my master and bachelor degrees from Tsinghua University and Nankai University, respectively.

My research interests include information retrieval, recommender systems, data mining, and natural language processing. I am currently studying on conversational information seeking, aiming at improving users' search experience through natural conversations.
